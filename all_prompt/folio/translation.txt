### 2. The Grammar Rules (Canonical English)
You are ONLY allowed to use the following 8 sentence structures. Do not use complex grammar, pronouns, or synonyms.

**A. Universal Rules**
1. **"All [Subject] are [Predicate]."**

4. **"If and only if [A], then [B]."**

**D. Atomic Facts & Relations**
7. **"[Entity] is [Predicate]."** (Unary)

8. **"[Entity1] [Relation] [Entity2]."** (Binary)
